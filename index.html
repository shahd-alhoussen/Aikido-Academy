Inner Strength

Beginners Training

Aikido training for beginners focuses on understanding movement, balance, and awareness. No prior experience or physical strength is required. Students learn how to use timing and body positioning instead of force, making aikido accessible to everyone. It also encourages cooperation and harmony with others during practice

Kids Training

Children’s aikido classes provide a safe and supportive environment where kids learn discipline, focus, and self-confidence. Through playful yet structured training, children develop body awareness, learn how to protect themselves using balance and smart movement rather than force, and cultivate teamwork and harmony with others

Adaptive Training (Special Needs)

This training program is tailored for individuals with physical challenges. Aikido techniques are adapted to suit different abilities, proving that effectiveness comes from understanding motion and balance, not strength. The goal is to build confidence, coordination, and personal empowerment. It also fosters collaboration and mutual support among participants

Academy Philosophy

Aikido is not based on physical strength or muscle power. It relies on understanding movement, balance, and natural weak points that cannot be strengthened. This philosophy allows practitioners of all ages, sizes, and abilities to train effectively and safely. The practice also promotes cooperation, respect, and harmony among all participants
